Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

ThrottleUp ("we", "us", "our") operates the ThrottleUp mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and authentication credentials when you create an account.
  • User-generated content: Items you create (motorcycles, maintenance records, parts, gear, modifications), including names, dates, mileage, costs, and notes.
  • Photos: Images you upload (motorcycle photos, receipts, VIN plates) are stored securely.
  • Device information: Push notification tokens for sending maintenance reminders.
  • Usage data: Basic app interaction data to improve the user experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (tracking motorcycles, maintenance, parts).
  • To send you push notifications and maintenance reminders.
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription.
  • To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on Supabase servers in the European Union (eu-central-1, Frankfurt). We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. Photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage with row-level security policies.

5. On-Device Processing

Our VIN scanning feature uses Google ML Kit for text recognition. All OCR processing happens entirely on your device — no images or text are sent to external servers for scanning.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and file storage (EU servers).
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase and subscription management.
  • Apple Push Notification Service / Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications.
  • Google ML Kit: On-device text recognition (no data leaves your device).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

7. Data Sharing

  • Garage groups: If you join a garage group, items assigned to that group are visible to all members.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at motolog@gmail.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

ThrottleUp is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification or email.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
motolog@gmail.com
